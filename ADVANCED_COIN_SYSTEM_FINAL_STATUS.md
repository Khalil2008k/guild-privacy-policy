### **1. Serialized Coins**
Every coin is a unique asset:
- Birth certificate (mint record)
- Life history (transfers)
- Death certificate (burn record)

### **2. Unhackable**
Even if someone tries:
- ❌ Invalid checksum → Rejected
- ❌ Missing from database → Quarantined
