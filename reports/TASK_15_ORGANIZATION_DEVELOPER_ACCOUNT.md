## üìù REQUIREMENTS FOR ORGANIZATION ACCOUNT

### **1. Legal Business Entity**

You need ONE of the following:

**Option A: Registered Company** ‚úÖ **RECOMMENDED**
- LLC, Corporation, Partnership, or equivalent
- Registered with government authorities
- Has a D-U-N-S Number (free from Dun & Bradstreet)
- Has a legal business address

**Option B: Sole Proprietorship**
- Registered business name
- Business license
- Tax identification number
- May require D-U-N-S Number

**Option C: Non-Profit Organization**
- 501(c)(3) or equivalent
- Tax-exempt status
- D-U-N-S Number

---

### **2. D-U-N-S Number** (Required)

**What is it:**
- Free unique 9-digit identifier for businesses
- Issued by Dun & Bradstreet
- Used by Apple to verify your organization

**How to get it:**
1. Go to: https://developer.apple.com/enroll/duns-lookup/
2. Search for your organization
3. If not found, request a new D-U-N-S Number (free)
4. Wait 1-2 weeks for processing

**Cost:** FREE  
**Time:** 1-2 weeks

---

### **3. Legal Authority**

You need to be ONE of:
- ‚úÖ Legal representative (CEO, Owner)
- ‚úÖ Authorized signatory
- ‚úÖ Have power of attorney

**Documents you may need:**
- Business registration certificate
- Articles of incorporation
- Business license
- Tax identification documents
- Proof of authority to bind the organization

---

### **4. Apple ID**

- Must be associated with your business email
- NOT a personal email (no gmail.com, yahoo.com, etc.)
- Example: admin@guildapp.com, developer@guildapp.com

---

### **5. Payment Method**

- Credit or debit card
- $99 USD annual fee
- Auto-renews yearly

## üöÄ STEP-BY-STEP SETUP GUIDE

### **PHASE 1: PREPARATION (1-2 weeks)**

#### **Step 1: Get D-U-N-S Number**

1. **Check if you already have one:**
   - Go to: https://developer.apple.com/enroll/duns-lookup/
   - Enter your organization name and address
   - If found, note your D-U-N-S Number

2. **If not found, request one:**
   - Click "Request a D-U-N-S Number"
   - Fill out the form with accurate information
   - Submit request
   - Wait 1-2 weeks for processing
   - You'll receive an email with your D-U-N-S Number

**Important:**
- ‚úÖ Use your LEGAL business name (exactly as registered)
- ‚úÖ Use your LEGAL business address
- ‚úÖ Provide accurate contact information
- ‚ùå Don't use abbreviations unless they're in your legal name
- ‚ùå Don't use PO Box addresses

---

#### **Step 2: Prepare Documents**

Gather these documents (you'll need them during enrollment):

**Required:**
- [ ] Business registration certificate
- [ ] D-U-N-S Number
- [ ] Legal business name and address
- [ ] Business website URL
- [ ] Business phone number
- [ ] Business email address

**May be required:**
- [ ] Articles of incorporation
- [ ] Business license
- [ ] Tax ID / EIN
- [ ] Proof of authority to sign on behalf of organization

---

#### **Step 3: Create Apple ID**

1. **Use business email:**
   - Example: developer@guildapp.com
   - NOT personal email

2. **Create Apple ID:**
   - Go to: https://appleid.apple.com/account
   - Click "Create Your Apple ID"
   - Use business email
   - Set strong password
   - Enable two-factor authentication (REQUIRED)

3. **Verify email:**
   - Check your business email
   - Click verification link

---

### **PHASE 2: ENROLLMENT (1-3 days)**

#### **Step 4: Start Enrollment**

1. **Go to Apple Developer:**
   - Visit: https://developer.apple.com/programs/enroll/
   - Click "Start Your Enrollment"

2. **Sign in:**
   - Use your business Apple ID
   - Complete two-factor authentication

3. **Choose Account Type:**
   - Select **"Organization"**
   - NOT "Individual"

---

#### **Step 5: Complete Organization Information**

**Legal Entity Information:**
```
Organization Name: [Your Legal Business Name]
D-U-N-S Number: [Your 9-digit D-U-N-S Number]
Organization Type: [LLC / Corporation / etc.]
Legal Address: [Your Business Address]
Phone: [Business Phone]
Website: [Your Business Website]
```

**Your Information (as legal representative):**
```
First Name: [Your First Name]
Last Name: [Your Last Name]
Job Title: [CEO / Owner / etc.]
Email: [Your Business Email]
Phone: [Your Phone]
```

**Important:**
- ‚úÖ All information must match your D-U-N-S record EXACTLY
- ‚úÖ Use legal business name (not DBA or trade name)
- ‚úÖ Double-check spelling and formatting

---

#### **Step 6: Accept License Agreement**

1. **Review Apple Developer Program License Agreement**
2. **Accept terms**
3. **Confirm you have authority to bind the organization**

---

#### **Step 7: Complete Purchase**

1. **Enter payment information:**
   - Credit or debit card
   - $99 USD

2. **Review order:**
   - Verify all information is correct

3. **Complete purchase:**
   - Click "Purchase"
   - You'll receive a confirmation email

---

#### **Step 8: Wait for Apple Verification**

**What happens:**
1. Apple reviews your application
2. Apple may call your business phone to verify
3. Apple may request additional documentation

**Timeline:**
- Usually: 24-48 hours
- Sometimes: Up to 1 week

**What to do:**
- ‚úÖ Answer phone calls from Apple (may show as California number)
- ‚úÖ Check email regularly for requests
- ‚úÖ Respond promptly to any verification requests

---

#### **Step 9: Verification Call (if required)**

**Apple may call to verify:**

- Contact information is correct

**Be prepared to:**
- ‚úÖ Confirm your organization name
- ‚úÖ Confirm your D-U-N-S Number
- ‚úÖ Confirm your role in the organization
- ‚úÖ Provide additional documentation if requested

   - Click "Users and Access"
   - Click "+" to add user

3. **Enter information:**
   - First name, last name
   - Email address
   - Select role(s)
   - Select app access

4. **Send invitation:**
   - User receives email invitation
   - User accepts and gains access

## ‚ö†Ô∏è COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS

### **Issue 1: D-U-N-S Number Not Found**
**Solution:**
- Request a new D-U-N-S Number (free)
- Wait 1-2 weeks
- Don't create duplicate requests

### **Issue 2: Information Mismatch**
**Solution:**
- Ensure all information matches D-U-N-S record EXACTLY
- Update D-U-N-S record if needed (can take 1-2 weeks)

### **Issue 3: Verification Delayed**
**Solution:**
- Check spam folder for Apple emails
- Answer phone calls from California area codes
- Respond promptly to verification requests

### **Issue 4: Authority to Sign**
**Solution:**
- Provide proof of authority (letter from CEO, board resolution, etc.)
- Have legal representative complete enrollment

### **Issue 5: Payment Declined**
**Solution:**
- Use different payment method
- Contact your bank to authorize international transaction
- Ensure sufficient funds ($99 USD)
