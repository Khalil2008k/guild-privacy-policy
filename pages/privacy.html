GUILD Privacy Policy
This privacy policy explains how GUILD ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Qatar odd jobs marketplace mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
  • Profile Data: Skills, work experience, location, availability
  • Job Information: Job postings, applications, work history
  • Payment Information: Payment methods, transaction history (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Communications: Messages, reviews, feedback

1.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of the app:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • App Usage: Features used, time spent, pages viewed
  • Location Data: Approximate location for job matching (with your consent)
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Services: Connect job seekers with employers, facilitate job matching
  • Communication: Send job notifications, updates, and important service messages
  • Payment Processing: Process payments securely through third-party providers
  • Safety and Security: Verify user identities, prevent fraud, ensure platform safety
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Support: Provide customer support and respond to inquiries

3. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Profile information visible to other users for job matching
  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data transmission encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Data stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security practices regularly reviewed and updated
Important: While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

6. Location Data

We collect location information to:

  • Show nearby job opportunities
  • Match users with local employers
  • Provide location-based services

You can control location sharing through your device settings. Location data is only collected with your explicit consent.

7. Children's Privacy

GUILD is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Qatar. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during international transfers.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Providing notice through the app

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@guild.qa
  • Support: support@guild.qa
  • Address: GUILD Privacy Team, Qatar
Response Time: We will respond to your privacy inquiries within 30 days.

11. Qatar-Specific Provisions

This privacy policy complies with Qatar's data protection laws and regulations. Users in Qatar have additional rights under local law, including the right to file complaints with relevant authorities.

This privacy policy is effective as of January 15, 2025, and was last updated on January 15, 2025.
