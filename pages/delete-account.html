Delete Account

⚠️ Warning

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. This action will:

  • Remove all your profile information
  • Delete all your projects and applications
  • Remove you from all guilds
  • Cancel any active subscriptions
  • Delete all your messages and communications

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the GUILD mobile app
  2. Go to Settings → Account Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision

Alternatively, you can contact our support team at support@blackenergytech.com to request account deletion.
