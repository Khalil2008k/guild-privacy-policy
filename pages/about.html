About GUILD

Qatar's Premier Odd Jobs Marketplace

Welcome to GUILD
GUILD is a revolutionary React Native marketplace app built specifically for the Qatar market, connecting job seekers with employers for odd jobs and freelance opportunities. Built with Expo, TypeScript, and NativeWind, GUILD offers full Arabic RTL support and a Qatar-themed design.

Our Mission

To create a seamless, accessible, and culturally appropriate platform that connects Qatar's diverse workforce with local job opportunities, fostering economic growth and community development.

What Makes GUILD Special

🌍 Arabic RTL Support

Full right-to-left layout with Noto Sans Arabic fonts, ensuring a native experience for Arabic-speaking users.

🎨 Qatar-Themed Design

Beautiful blue (#1E90FF) and white color scheme that reflects Qatar's national colors and cultural identity.

🔥 Firebase Integration

Robust backend with Firestore and Realtime Database for secure data storage and real-time chat functionality.

📱 Cross-Platform

Native iOS and Android support with Expo, ensuring consistent experience across all devices.

♿ Accessibility First

High-contrast text, 48x48dp touch targets, and full screen reader support for inclusive design.

🌐 Internationalization

Arabic and English localization with Islamic calendar support and Arabic number formats.

Our Technology Stack

Frontend Technologies

  • React Native: Mobile app framework for cross-platform development
  • Expo: Development platform and build tools
  • TypeScript: Type safety and enhanced developer experience
  • NativeWind: Tailwind CSS for React Native styling

Backend & Services

  • Firebase: Backend services including Firestore, Realtime Database, and Authentication
  • i18n-js: Internationalization and localization
  • expo-localization: Date and number formatting
  • expo-font: Custom font loading for Arabic support

Our Commitment to Qatar

GUILD is built with deep respect for Qatar's culture, values, and regulatory environment:

  • Cultural Sensitivity: Design and features that respect local customs and traditions
  • Regulatory Compliance: Full compliance with Qatar's labor and data protection laws
  • Local Support: Customer support available in both Arabic and English
  • Economic Impact: Supporting local businesses and freelancers in Qatar's growing economy

Accessibility Features

GUILD is designed to be accessible to all users:

  • High Contrast Text: Dark text on light backgrounds for better readability
  • 48x48dp Touch Targets: Minimum touch target size for easy interaction
  • Screen Reader Support: Proper accessibility labels and descriptions
  • Keyboard Navigation: Full keyboard support for all features
  • Voice Commands: Voice control support for hands-free operation
