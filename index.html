Introduction

Welcome to GUILD. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application and services.

By using GUILD, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1Privacy Overview

GUILD is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using GUILD, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture, and professional information
  • Purpose: To create and manage your account, enable authentication, and facilitate communication
  • Storage: Securely stored in Firebase Authentication and Firestore

2.2 Usage Data

  • Activity Information: Pages visited, features used, session duration, and interaction patterns
  • Purpose: To understand user behavior, improve services, and optimize user experience
  • Collection Method: Firebase Analytics

2.3 Device Information

  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system, browser information, app version, and IP address
  • Purpose: Technical support, app optimization, and security monitoring
  • Usage: Aggregated for performance analysis

2.4 Location Data

  • Approximate Location: City/region from IP address or user-provided location
  • GPS Coordinates: Only when searching for nearby jobs (with your permission)
  • Important: We do NOT track your location continuously. Location is only used when the app is open and you're actively searching for jobs

2.5 Payment Information

  • Transaction Data: Guild Coins balance and transaction history
  • Payment Details: Processed by secure third-party payment processors (we do NOT store credit card information)
  • Current Status: Guild Coins system (beta) - no real payment data collected yet

2.6 User-Generated Content

  • Profile Content: Bio, skills, work history, portfolio items
  • Job Data: Jobs posted, applications submitted, guild memberships
  • Communications: Chat messages, file attachments, timestamps
  • Media: Profile photos, job-related images, documents

3How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide and improve our services, including job matching, guild functionality, and professional networking
  • Communication: To communicate with you about your account, transactions, platform updates, and job opportunities
  • Security: To ensure platform security, prevent fraud, detect abuse, and protect user safety
  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns, understand user preferences, and improve user experience
  • Personalization: To customize content and recommendations based on your interests and activity
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

5Data Storage & Retention

  • Security Measures: Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption (AES-256), SSL/TLS for transmission, and secure cloud infrastructure (Firebase/Google Cloud)
  • Retention Period: We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
  • Account Deletion: You can request deletion of your data at any time. Data will be deleted within 30 days of account closure
  • Backup Data: Some data may remain in backups for up to 90 days but will not be accessible or used
  • Legal Obligations: We may retain certain data to comply with legal, regulatory, or contractual requirements

6Cookies & Tracking Technologies

  • Purpose: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, remember preferences, and analyze platform usage
  • Types: Session cookies (temporary), persistent cookies (saved preferences), analytics cookies (usage tracking)
  • Control: You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences
  • Impact: Some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled

7Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information through account settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications and non-essential data collection
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for specific purposes

How to Exercise Your Rights: Contact us at privacy@guild.qa or use the in-app privacy settings.
